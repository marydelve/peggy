Features

The site's features allow you to find out why security is so important in the field.

Whois Lookup

Without knowing who you are, the world wide web knows where you are. Every time you access to the internet, information releases on the network that could be used by an attacker to trick you.

Hack your Site

Have you developed a website but do not know how to protect it? This feature allows you to test the security of your site, analyzing the critical issues that hackers use to steal information.

Email: Fake or Safe?

You have received an email but do not know who you sent her? It could really be your bank, your friend or a distant relative. What are the parameters that determine the reliability of an email? Find out!
